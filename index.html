Who We Are

A Trusted OEM Partner for the Manufacturing Sector

Founded in 2021 under the leadership of Mr. Vinayak Patil (B.Tech Mechanical Engineering, 15+ years industry expertise), VP Machine Technologies is a proprietary manufacturing enterprise based in the Udyambag Industrial Estate, Belagavi — one of Karnataka's most active industrial corridors.

We produce high-precision components that meet international quality standards (IS, DIN, BS, ISO, ASTM), serving major clients in agriculture, automotive, and heavy engineering sectors.
