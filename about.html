Our Journey

Scale & Engineering Focus

Recognizing a gap in the market for high-precision, reliable component manufacturing, VP Machine Technologies was founded in 2021 with a clear vision: to deliver world-class engineering solutions directly to heavy industries and agricultural developers.

Operating from the industrial manufacturing hub of Belagavi, North Karnataka, we have rapidly scaled our manufacturing footprints to become a trusted, ISO-certified Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) partner.

Our state-of-the-art facility specializes in producing precision parts with tight dimensional control, robust industrial steel fabrications, and customized Special Service Tools (SST) designed specifically for major tractor and agricultural enterprises.
