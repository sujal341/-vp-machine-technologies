Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

The types of cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly (e.g., theme preference, session state)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our website (aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Performance cookies — enable features like page speed improvements
🍪 You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted technology or hosting partners who assist in operating our website (bound by confidentiality)
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business protection — to protect the rights, property, or safety of VP Machine Technologies or others
  • With your consent — in any other case, only with your explicit prior approval

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and use it only for the specific purpose for which it was shared.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Our general retention guidelines are:

  • Inquiry and quotation records — retained for up to 3 years from the date of last contact
  • Customer order and business records — retained for up to 7 years as required by Indian tax and commercial law
  • Website analytics data — retained for up to 12 months in aggregated, anonymized form

Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, or disclosure. These include:

  • Secure HTTPS connections for all website communications
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for internal data systems
  • Regular review of our data collection and storage practices
  • Staff awareness of data privacy obligations
🔒 While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you not to share sensitive personal information through unsecured channels.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer needed

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at vpmachinetechnologies@gmail.com. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.
